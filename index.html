05 / Program Economics

Dominion Energy can change the project math.

Program structure

50% make-ready incentive Dominion describes the incentive as applying to EV charging construction and installation, commonly referred to as make-ready.
Minimum two qualifying stations Eligibility generally requires a Virginia commercial or industrial Dominion customer, decision authority for the site, at least two qualifying stations, a program contract, and site readiness.
ChargePoint and Tesla options Dominion’s program materials reference ChargePoint and Tesla options when Dominion installs and maintains the charging stations.
Customer control of use and pricing Site hosts can set access controls and usage fees through the charging provider, which lets Aperture manage resident access, overstays, and cost recovery.

Program description summarized from Dominion Energy’s public Level 2 Charging Program materials. Final eligibility, pricing, approved hardware, and contract terms should be confirmed with Dominion Energy before approval.
